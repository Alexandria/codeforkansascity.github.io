What can I work on?

We have a few projects planned, but need your ideas, too! We have already engaged Historic Kansas City, the City of KCMO, Community Capital Fund and others willing to support our projects. We just need designers, organizers, doers, and coders like you to lend a hand and a day or two to bring these ideas (and others) to life! Here are some examples of apps we can build together:

Projects

Neighborhood Dashboard
Help develop a dashboard and map for neighborhoods that aggregates existing data and information into a single, easy-to-read site. This may also have features to create monthly reports of activities in a particular neighborhood. For example, what businesses are opening or closing? What opportunities are there for redevelopment, what Land Bank properties are available. What upcoming construction projects/demolitions will affect the neighborhood? Are there zoning and code changes that would effect your neighborhood? Is your street going to be closed for repairs? We’d like to build on the work Code for KC has done with the CommunityKC map for neighborhoods and nonprofits to provide even more resources for those working to improve our neighborhoods and quality of life.

Team: Amanda or Community Capital Fund, Leslie

Public Transit App
You are standing at a bus stop. When will the next bus come – and where could you go on it? Wouldn't it be great if you could use your smartphone to quickly access information customized to your current location.? Let's improve commuter education while promoting digital information usage.

SafeAssist
SafeAssist is a collaborative effort initiated by the Kansas City Police Department to provide officers with information that can aid them in more effectively managing contacts with diminished capacity persons. Every year the Kansas City Police Department contacts hundreds of individuals with physical and behavioral health issues. Officers are often at a disadvantage in handling these types of service calls due to lack of information or communication impediments. SafeAssist was created to provide a vehicle for caregivers and individuals to share pertinent health and behavioral information to help both officers and individuals process the needs of those service calls much more effectively, while increasing the overall quality of service.

Project Lead:Mike Grigsby, Director Information Services KCPD

WikiKC Improvements and Additions
WikiKC.org is a living e-document pertaining to all things KC, that anyone can contribute to or edit. This will be used by anyone interested in KC or in contributing to the content. WikiKC provides an ongoing depot for posting information and telling neighborhood stories.

Project Lead:Synthia

Parcel Assessment Tool
P.A.T. is designed to allow those with an interest in real estate to quickly assess if a piece of land is suitable for their project. The P.A.T.'s GIS system allows users to pinpoint locations, access the current zoning information for the space, then perform calculations to determine the specifications of an appropriate building.

Team: Ryan, Ron
