SCHEDULE

Overview

Saturday you will:

  • Get your team together
  • Define the problem and find the best solution
  • Start on the design and coding of your solution

Sunday you will:

  • Create a road map for the project
  • Present your work completed

In July teams will present their progress, to date, at an all-hands meeting. Date TBD.

In October/November final presentations will be made along with a shared celebration. Date TBD.

Join local designers, developers, doers and thinkers with a passion for making local government work better through technology.

Details

Saturday June 6th

  • 10:00 - Registration - Arrive and get checked in
  • 10:30 - Initial Talks, including Welcomes from Dean Ellen Sunni and Kansas City Chief Innovation Officer Ashley Hand
  • 10:45 - Project Pitches - 5 minutes for each team leader to pitch their project, plus open pitches
  • 11:00 Form Teams - This is the opportunity for those that are not on a team to find a team they can join.
  • 11:15 - Ice Breaker
  • 11:30 Start Work - Teams will take evaluate the skills they have, start discussing, designing and planning.
  • 12:00 - Working lunch provided
  • 12:00-8:00pm - Open hacking
  • 8:30pm - Wrap up for day

Sunday June 7th

  • 10:00am - Open hacking & dev
  • 12:00pm - Working lunch provided
  • 3:00pm - Team presentations & demos
  • 4:00pm - Awards presented for Most Innovative, Best Use of Public Data, Most Likely to Make Big Impact, and Best Story
  • 5:00pm - Wrap up & next steps
